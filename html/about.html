Our story

— Every solution starts with a problem...

Real estate data is plentiful and comes from many places, and OREEVA’s machine learning algorithm can handle all of it with aplomb. OREEVA stands for Online Real Estate Estimates, Valuations & Analytics, and we do just that. We offer full transparency to the real estate market, at a low cost, and at the level of individual postcodes. And we help our clients save time and money when evaluating a property, assessing risk or reevaluating their portfolios.

— The team that does it all...

One of our greatest accomplishments has been assembling a group of experienced and driven individuals who comprise our core team and our advisers. Each one has mastered his or her domain, be it computer science, deep learning or real estate investing. We’re all excited about building the best possible product and introducing it to the real estate market. And we’re thrilled at the prospect of helping you do what you do best, which is making the right investment for the right place at the right time.
